# Balance

Every boss fight is designed to have fair attack patterns. Most boss fight should take 3-5 minutes, and each boss fight should be no-hittable as melee with slightly above average speed. The game is balanced with a speed of `15 tiles/s` as the minimum speed, `20 tiles/s` as the average speed, `25 tiles/s` as the no-hit speed, and `35 tiles/s` as the maximum speed. There's no hard limit, though.

Each class (sword, dagger, staff, wand, and bow) has different strengths and weaknesses that hopefully make them all feel the same strength, although melee usually ends up as the most broken class.

`sword` - Balanced around high attack, defense, vitality, and stamina. They have to get close to do damage but are also the tankiest.
`dagger` - Balanced around speed, attack speed, crit, and stamina. They are good for burst damage.
`bow` - Balanced around speed, damage, attack speed, and stamina. They are good for consistent damage at distance.
`staff` - Balanced around damage, ability haste, attack speed, and mana. They are good for consistent damage at distance.
`wand` - Balanced around ability haste, wisdom, intelligence, and mana. They are good for consistent ability damage.
